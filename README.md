# quickhire-
QuickHire is an online service marketplace platform designed specifically for the Ghanaian market. It connects customers who need household and professional services with verified, local service providers. Unlike traditional e-commerce platforms that sell physical products, QuickHire operates as an e-business platform facilitating the entire service transaction lifecycle, from discovery and booking through payment and review.
The platform addresses a real problem in Ghana: finding reliable, trustworthy service providers is largely word-of-mouth, inefficient, and offers no consumer protection. QuickHire digitises this process, providing transparency, accountability, and convenience for both customers and providers.
